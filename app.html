App Information

Welcome to the Construction Department App!

Here you'll use the app for course news, logging evidence and exporting evidence to tutors online!

Instructions on how to use the app:

To use the app, you can create an account on the app itself and then log in, there you'll be greeted with different pages, you'll be using the Entries to log your evidence for your class assignments, and then you'll be able save and export them to peers or tutors.

The app also allows the user to set their preffered username and profile picture, and are able to log out of their accounts

You can use the app down below, or use the embedded link to the app itself.
